Welcome to the Research Database

The Research Database is an Open Source software application aimed at providing historians with a database on the following types of data:

Artefacts
Details of when and where found, by whom, description of the artefact
Bibliographies and Publications
Details of the author(s) and publishers, when written and description
Places, countries and regions
Deities
Details of deities, religions and peoples that they were associated with
Hierarchies - for modelling religious
Details of ecclesiastical, military, political or other forms of hierarchy
Languages and Language Groups
Materials
Measurements
Measurements and convertors
People
Details of people, where and when born and died, roles carried out, titles and attributes
Places, countries and regions
Technologies and groups of technologies
Theories
